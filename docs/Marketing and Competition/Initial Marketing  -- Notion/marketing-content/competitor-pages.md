You love the envelope method. You hate that it costs $109/year.

YNAB started as a $60 one-time purchase. Then it moved to $50/year. Then $84. Now $109. That's an 82% price increase in six years, and the community frustration is loud. Monarch Money gets 37+ direct mentions as the top alternative in YNAB forums.
