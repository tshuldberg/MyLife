## 1. Strava

**Category:** Social fitness tracking for running, cycling, hiking, and 50+ sport types
**Users:** 180M+ registered athletes
**Revenue:** ~$500M ARR (IPO filing anticipated)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web, Apple Watch, Wear OS
**Pricing:**
- Free tier with core tracking
- Individual: $11.99/month or $79.99/year
- Family Plan: $139.99/year (up to 4 accounts)
- Student: $39.99/year (SheerID verified)
- Strava + Runna bundle: $149.99/year

### Activity Recording & GPS Tracking

- **GPS activity recording** with real-time map and stats displayed simultaneously (no screen switching during workouts)
- **50+ supported sport types** including Run, Ride, Swim, Hike, Walk, Alpine Ski, Backcountry Ski, Snowboard, Trail Run, Mountain Bike, Gravel Bike, E-Bike, Hand Cycle, Wheelchair, CrossFit, Elliptical, Stair Stepper, Weight Training, Yoga, HIIT, Pilates, Tennis, Pickleball, Padel, Basketball, Volleyball, Cricket, Dance, Racquetball, Squash, Badminton, Table Tennis, Virtual Ride, Virtual Run, and more
- **Indoor activity recording** for stationary bikes, treadmills, weight training, yoga, CrossFit, elliptical, stair stepper, and generic workouts
- **Real-time splits** updating live during movement for smarter pacing
- **Live Laps** for marking laps at specific distances or intervals during a workout
- **Winter sport tracking** with automatic chairlift time removal, detailed trail/lift/terrain maps, and live elevation tracking across all winter sports
- **Perceived Exertion** manual intensity rating (1-10 scale) for activities without heart rate monitors
- **Activity tags** for categorizing and organizing completed activities

### Segments & Leaderboards

- **Segments** define specific portions of routes (climbs, trail sections, road stretches) with automatic time and speed recording each time completed
- **Segment Leaderboards** showing KOM (King of Mountain), QOM (Queen of Mountain), and CR (Course Record) rankings
- **Filtered Leaderboards** (Premium) filter by time period, followers, clubs, age group, and weight class
- **Live Segments** (Premium) real-time performance display during activity with comparison to personal record and current KOM/QOM/CR
- **Matched Activities** benchmark current performance against past performances on frequently used routes

### Training & Analysis

- **Relative Effort** measures cardiovascular work from heart rate data or Perceived Exertion, with week-over-week trend graph showing cumulative training load
- **Fitness & Freshness** (Premium) tracks three metrics over time using impulse-response model (Dr. Banister, 1975):
  - Fitness Score: training load averaged over 42 days
  - Fatigue Score: training load averaged over 7 days
  - Form Score: difference between fitness and fatigue (peaking indicator)
- **Training Log** central view of all training, filterable by sport type, time, distance, elevation, or relative effort
- **Heart Rate Zones** categorized from Zone 1 (active recovery) to Zone 5 (near-maximum effort), auto-calculated from estimated max HR by age or customizable
- **Power Curve** (cycling) maximum power sustained over time intervals, revealing rider type, strengths, and weaknesses
- **Training Plans** (Premium) structured plans for running and cycling
- **Instant Workouts** (Premium, 2026) AI-generated personalized workouts across 40+ sport types with four intent modes: Maintain, Build, Explore, Recover. GPS workouts include auto-generated routes powered by Heatmap data
- **Custom Goals** (Premium) weekly, monthly, annual, segment, and power goals for select sport types

### Routes & Navigation

- **Suggested Routes** (Premium) AI-generated personalized routes from any starting location
- **Personal Heatmaps** (Premium) visualization of all ground covered worldwide
- **Global Heatmaps** (Premium) showing where millions of athletes run and ride (brighter lines = more popular)
- **3D Terrain** (Premium) map layer for elevation visualization
- **Winter Map** (Premium) specialized map style for snow sports
- **Route Builder** create and save custom routes on the web
- **Navigation** turn-by-turn directions during activities

### Social & Community

- **Activity Feed** stream of activities, posts, and adventures from followed athletes
- **Kudos** quick thumbs-up recognition for fellow athletes' activities
- **Comments** on activities, posts, achievements, challenges, and goals
- **Clubs** create or join community groups with club leaderboards on segments, routes, distance, and elevation
- **Club Events** organized group activities within clubs
- **Group Activities** automatic detection when multiple users recorded activities at the same time on the same route, linking matching activities
- **Flyby** playback tool showing your activity alongside nearby athletes on a synchronized map and timeline
- **Athlete Profiles** with activity history, stats, and social connections
- **Activity Sharing** post activities with photos, videos, maps, and stats
- **Beacon** (Premium, free on app) real-time location sharing with designated safety contacts during activities
- **Challenges** (Premium) group challenges for motivation and engagement

### Wearable & Device Integration

- **Apple Watch** standalone app, all models from Series 3+, Apple Watch Ultra, Apple Watch SE
- **Garmin** automatic sync via Garmin Connect (all current models including Forerunner, Fenix, Enduro, Venu)
- **Wahoo** cycling computers and sensors
- **Polar** watches and heart rate monitors (H10, Vantage, Grit X, Pacer)
- **COROS** GPS watches (Pace, Apex, Vertix)
- **Suunto** sports watches
- **Bryton** cycling computers
- **Fitbit** activity tracker sync
- **Wear OS 3.0+** devices
- **Bluetooth heart rate monitors** (Polar H10, Wahoo TICKR, Garmin HRM)
- **Bluetooth speed/cadence sensors** pairable directly with Strava app
- **Power meters** for cycling power data
- **Third-party app sync** including Fitbod, Aaptiv, Sworkit, Barre3, Peloton, Zwift, and more

### Data & Analytics

- **Year in Sport** annual activity summary and statistics
- **Activity statistics** including distance, pace/speed, elevation gain, moving time, elapsed time, calories burned, average/max heart rate
- **Split analysis** per-mile or per-kilometer breakdowns
- **Elevation profile** for route analysis
- **Photo and video attachments** on activities

## 2. Fitbod

**Category:** AI-powered personalized strength training
**Revenue:** ~$20M ARR
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Apple Watch, Web
**Pricing:**
- Free: 3 workouts to try
- Premium: $12.99-$15.99/month or $79.99-$95.99/year (varies by platform)

### AI Workout Generation

- **Personalized workout algorithm** creates daily workouts based on training history, available equipment, muscle group recovery status, and fitness goals
- **Adaptive AI** learns from every logged set, rep, and rest interval, adjusting future recommendations based on actual performance
- **Non-linear periodization** automatically varies workout structure (sets, reps, intensity) across sessions for sustained progress
- **Progressive overload intelligence** automatically increases weights, reps, or sets to ensure continuous strength and muscle gains
- **Four training goal modes** for muscle building, strength, endurance, or general fitness
- **Three fitness levels** (beginner, intermediate, advanced) with appropriate exercise selection and loading
- **Workout modifiers** to adjust generated sessions before starting (swap exercises, change volume)
- **Recovery-based programming** tracks individual muscle recovery and prioritizes fresh muscle groups for next session

### Muscle Recovery System

- **Muscle recovery percentages** (0-100%) for each muscle group based on training history, with full recovery at 6 days
- **Muscle fatigue heat map** visual body map showing which muscles are fresh (green) vs fatigued (red)
- **mStrength scores** per-muscle-group strength metric on a relative scale based on exercise history
- **Overall Strength Score** aggregated body-wide strength metric averaging all mStrength scores

### Exercise Library

- **1,000+ exercises** with HD multi-angle video demonstrations
- **Detailed exercise instructions** with form cues and technique guidance
- **Search by muscle group, equipment type, or keyword**
- **Equipment categories** including barbell, dumbbell, kettlebell, cable machine, smith machine, TRX, resistance bands, Bosu ball, EZ bar, trap bar, bodyweight, and more
- **Customizable equipment settings** so workouts only include exercises for available equipment
- **Dynamic warm-ups and cooldowns** integrated into each session
- **Optional mobility and stretching suggestions** for recovery enhancement

### Workout Logging & Tracking

- **Set-by-set logging** of weight, reps, and rest time
- **Workout history** with all past sessions searchable and reviewable
- **Calories burned estimation** using workout data and heart rate (when available)
- **Workout streaks** tracking consecutive training days
- **Total volume tracking** (weight x reps x sets) per session and over time
- **Progress analytics** with visual charts showing strength progression

### Body & Progress Tracking

- **Bodyweight tracking** with trend visualization
- **Body measurement logging** for circumference measurements
- **Progress photos** for visual comparison over time

### Integrations

- **Apple Health** bidirectional sync for workouts, heart rate, body measurements
- **Apple Watch** standalone workout tracking with heart rate monitoring, calorie calculation, and auto-sync to Apple Fitness
- **Strava** post-workout sync
- **Fitbit** integration

### Programs & Structure

- **Workout scheduling** set preferred training days per week
- **Muscle group focus** specify which body parts to target or avoid
- **Equipment profiles** save different equipment sets for home gym vs commercial gym

## 3. Hevy

**Category:** Workout tracker and gym log with social features
**Users:** 5M+ registered users
**Revenue:** ~$6M ARR (bootstrapped)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Apple Watch, Wear OS, Web (desktop)
**Pricing:**
- Free: Unlimited workout logging, limited analytics and routines
- Hevy Pro: $3.99/month or $39.99/year
- Hevy Coach: Separate pricing for personal trainers ($19.99-$74.99/month based on client count)

### Workout Logging

- **Set-by-set logging** with reps, weight, and RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion)
- **Start Empty Workout** begin sessions without predefined structure
- **Previous Workout Values** reference historical data during logging for easy progressive overload
- **Warm-up sets** tracked separately from working sets
- **Drop sets** designated set type for intensity techniques
- **Failure sets** mark sets taken to muscular failure
- **Supersets** group exercises to be performed back-to-back
- **Automatic Rest Timer** configurable countdown between sets
- **Weight Plate Calculator** shows exact plate combinations needed for target weight
- **Warm-up Set Calculator** (Pro) generates warm-up progression to working weight
- **RPE tracking** rate perceived exertion on each set
- **Custom Exercise Notes** add text notes to individual exercises
- **Live Personal Record Notification** real-time alerts when a new PR is achieved during workout
- **Live Activity** (iOS) shows active workout status on lock screen and Dynamic Island
- **Saving a Workout** auto-save completed sessions to history

### Exercise Library & Customization

- **400+ built-in exercises** with form cues and video demonstrations
- **Custom exercise creation** define personal exercises with name, muscle groups, equipment type
- **Exercise categories** by muscle group, equipment, and movement pattern
- **Unlimited custom exercises** (Pro, limited on free)

### Routines & Programs

- **Routine templates** save and reuse workout structures
- **Routine folders** organize templates into categorized collections
- **Routine Library** pre-built workout programs including body-part splits, beginner programs, and equipment-specific workouts
- **Share folders and routines** send workout programs to other Hevy users via link
- **Unlimited routine templates** (Pro, limited to 3 on free)

### AI Features

- **HevyGPT** custom ChatGPT integration that generates tailored workout programs from natural language prompts and imports them directly into Hevy (free users limited to 4-routine plans)
- **Hevy Trainer** automated workout plan generation based on goals and experience

### Progress Tracking & Analytics

- **Exercise performance charts** track weight, reps, volume, and estimated 1RM over time
- **Muscle Group Workout Chart** visual breakdown of training distribution
- **Sets Per Muscle Group Per Week** weekly volume tracking by body part
- **Muscle Distribution Chart** analyze training balance across muscle groups
- **Muscle heatmap** (Pro) body visualization showing trained vs untrained muscles
- **Personal records** automatic detection and celebration of PRs across all exercises
- **Estimated 1RM tracking** calculated max based on logged performance
- **Volume per session** total weight moved per workout
- **Workout consistency and streaks** attendance patterns and consecutive workout tracking
- **Full graph history** (Pro, free limited to last 3 months)
- **Monthly Report** automated monthly fitness summary
- **Year in Review** annual summary of fitness accomplishments

### Body Measurements

- **Body weight tracking** with trend graphs
- **Body fat percentage** (Pro) logging and tracking
- **Body part circumference** (Pro) measurement tracking for arms, chest, waist, etc.
- **Progress photos** store and compare transformation images over time

### Social Features

- **Social Feed** activity stream showing friends' workouts
- **Discovery Feed** explore workouts from the broader community
- **Follow system** follow friends and athletes
- **Athlete/User Profiles** customizable profiles with workout history and stats
- **Workout likes and comments** engage with others' training
- **Leaderboards** competitive rankings among connections
- **Compare Performance and Stats** head-to-head comparison with friends
- **Social Media Shareables** generate branded workout summary images for Instagram, etc.
- **Upload Media** attach photos and videos to workouts
- **Strava Integration** auto-post workouts to Strava

### Coach Platform (Hevy Coach)

- **Client management** add and manage clients from a coach dashboard
- **Assign workouts** create and push workout programs to clients
- **Chat with clients** in-app messaging between coach and athlete
- **Track client progress** view client workout history and analytics
- **Client app** clients use the standard Hevy app with coach-assigned workouts

### Wearable Integration

- **Apple Watch** standalone workout tracking (no phone needed), heart rate monitoring, auto-sync when reconnected
- **Wear OS** standalone workout logging on Android smartwatches with heart rate and calorie tracking
- **Apple Watch custom watch faces** for quick workout access
- **Heart rate data** captured from wearables during workouts
- **Calorie burn estimation** using heart rate data from connected wearables

### Settings & Customization

- **12 workout settings** configurable preferences for workout behavior
- **Home screen widgets** (iOS/Android) quick-access workout widgets
- **Cardio tracking** support for treadmill, cycling, rowing, and other cardio modalities
- **HIIT support** interval-based workout logging
- **Bodyweight/calisthenics tracking** log bodyweight exercises with reps only

## 4. JEFIT

**Category:** Exercise database and community workout platform
**Users:** 12M+ registered users
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Apple Watch, Wear OS, Web
**Pricing:**
- Free: Core features with ads, limited analytics
- Elite Monthly: $12.99/month
- Elite Yearly: $69.99/year ($5.83/month)
- No free trial, no lifetime option

### Exercise Library

- **1,400-1,500+ exercises** covering weightlifting, bodyweight strength, and cardio
- **HD video demonstrations** with detailed form tutorials for every movement
- **Exercise search** by muscle group, equipment type, movement pattern, or keyword
- **Exercise tips** (Elite) expert guidance for each exercise
- **BodyMap Exercise Breakdown** visual display showing which muscles each exercise targets and contribution percentage

### Workout Planning

- **Custom workout plan builder** with drag-and-drop exercise arrangement
- **850+ pre-built workout plans** from the JEFIT community and expert trainers
- **Community routine database** download daily routines and fitness plans created by other users
- **One-tap Instant Workouts** (Elite, limited free) quick workout generation by muscle group, fitness level, or available time
- **Superset support** group exercises for back-to-back performance
- **Circuit training routines** create timed circuit-style workouts
- **Interval training** built-in support for timed intervals
- **Rest timer** configurable rest periods between sets
- **Equipment-based filtering** build workouts around available equipment

### Progress Tracking

- **1 Rep Max (1RM) estimation** automatically calculated from logged performance
- **Advanced 1RM analytics** (Elite) detailed strength trends over time
- **Strength performance tracking** monitor progression on individual exercises
- **Performance graphs** for each exercise showing weight, reps, and volume trends
- **Total volume and tonnage** per workout tracking
- **Training reports** (Elite) comprehensive workout summaries and insights
- **Workout history** complete log of all past sessions
- **Compare records with friends** (Elite) head-to-head performance comparison

### Body Measurements & Progress

- **Body weight tracking** log and chart weight over time
- **Body fat percentage** measurement logging
- **Circumference measurements** track arm, chest, waist, leg, and other body part measurements
- **Progress photos** store and compare transformation images
- **Height tracking** basic body stats

### BodyMap Feature

- **Visual muscle map** showing trained muscle groups and training volume distribution
- **Exercise breakdown by muscle** see contribution percentages for each exercise
- **Training balance visualization** identify over/under-trained muscle groups

### Community & Social

- **Global community** with 12M+ users sharing workout plans and progress
- **Workout plan sharing** upload and download community-created routines
- **Social accountability** share goals and fitness journeys
- **Workout videos** upload training videos to activities for form review and PR celebrations
- **Community challenges** participate in group fitness goals

### Integrations

- **Apple Health** sync workout data, body weight, height, and exercise time
- **Strava** post-workout activity sync
- **Smartwatch support** (Apple Watch, Wear OS) log workouts from wrist
- **Unlimited smartwatch workouts** (Elite, limited on free)
- **Audio cues** (Elite) voice prompts during workouts

### Analytics (Elite)

- **Detailed charts and analytics** comprehensive training data visualization
- **Training frequency analysis** workout consistency tracking
- **Deeper fitness stats** advanced metrics beyond basic logging
- **Exercise performance trends** long-term progression analysis

## 5. Strong

**Category:** Clean, minimalist workout logger for serious lifters
**Users:** 3M+ users
**Rating:** 4.9 stars (App Store)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Apple Watch, Web
**Pricing:**
- Free: Unlimited workout logging, limited to 3 custom routines
- Strong Pro: $4.99/month or $29.99/year

### Workout Logging

- **Quick set logging** input weight and reps with minimal taps
- **Superset/grouped exercises** combine exercises for back-to-back performance
- **RPE tracking** Rate of Perceived Exertion on individual sets for training intensity measurement
- **Warm-up set tracking** separate warm-up sets from working sets with different styling
- **Auto rest timer** configurable countdown between sets with audio/haptic alerts
- **Custom timers** set different rest durations per exercise or globally
- **Previous workout reference** see last session's numbers while logging for easy progressive overload
- **Workout notes** add text notes to workouts or individual exercises

### Exercise Library

- **Built-in exercise database** with comprehensive cardio and strength exercises
- **Animated video demonstrations** with form guidance for exercises
- **Custom exercise creation** add exercises with name, muscle group, and category
- **Exercise categorization** by muscle group, equipment, and type
- **Unlimited custom exercises** (Pro, limited on free)

### Routines & Templates

- **Routine templates** save workout structures for quick repeat access
- **Unlimited routines** (Pro, free limited to 3)
- **Optimized for barbell programs** works particularly well with Starting Strength, StrongLifts 5x5, and similar progressive programs
- **Adaptable to any routine** flexible enough for bodybuilding splits, PPL, upper/lower, or custom programs

### Progress Tracking & Analytics

- **Personal records** automatic PR detection and tracking across all exercises
- **Volume tracking** per session, per exercise, per muscle group
- **Estimated 1RM** calculated from logged performance data
- **Advanced charts** (Pro) detailed progression graphs for weight, reps, and volume over time
- **Muscle group volume analysis** visualize training distribution
- **Workout history** complete searchable log of all past sessions
- **Daily bodyweight tracker** log bodyweight with trend visualization

### Body Measurements

- **Body part measurements** track circumferences for arms, chest, waist, legs, and more
- **Weight tracking** daily bodyweight logging with trend graphs
- **Apple Health sync** (Pro) bidirectional body measurement data exchange

### Integrations & Platform

- **Apple Watch** fully-featured companion app for logging workouts without phone, with real-time sync when both devices present
- **Apple Health** (Pro) sync workouts, heart rate, body measurements
- **Siri Shortcuts** quick actions for starting workouts via voice
- **3rd party integrations** connect with other fitness platforms
- **CSV export** export all workout data for external analysis
- **Dark mode** system-wide dark theme support
- **Workout sharing** share completed workouts with others
- **Cloud backup** (Pro) automatic data backup and cross-device sync

### Specialized Training Features

- **Plate calculator** (Pro) shows exact plates needed per side for target weight
- **Warm-up calculator** (Pro) generates warm-up set progression to working weight
- **Advanced periodization support** structure for percentage-based and undulating programs
- **RPE-based training support** for autoregulated programming methodologies
