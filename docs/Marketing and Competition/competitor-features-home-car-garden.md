Comprehensive feature analysis for MyHomes, MyCar, and potential MyGarden module positioning.

## REAL ESTATE APPS

### Zillow

**Company:** Zillow Group | **Revenue:** $2.6B | **Monthly Users:** 221M | **Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web

#### Search and Discovery

- **Map-Based Listings:** Interactive map with colored pins for each listing. Tap a pin to see home details, price, and photos. Supports satellite, street, and standard map views.
- **Advanced Search Filters:** Filter by price range, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, lot size, year built, home type (house, condo, townhouse, multi-family, manufactured, land, apartment), and more.
- **Granular Filters:** Views, amenities (air conditioning, pool, garage, fireplace, basement, waterfront), upcoming open houses, keywords, days on Zillow, parking spots, HOA fees, and stories.
- **Saved Searches:** Save any search with custom filters. Configure email alert frequency (instant, daily, weekly) for new matching listings.
- **Recently Viewed:** Track properties you have browsed for easy revisiting.
- **Messaging (2025):** In-app messaging for buyers, co-shoppers, and agents to share listings, collaborate, and stay organized within the Zillow app.
- **Smart Search (Natural Language):** Search with conversational phrases like "ranch-style house with a pool in Austin."

#### Property Data and Valuation

- **Zestimate:** AI-powered home valuation for nearly every U.S. property, incorporating county/tax assessor records, MLS data, and home facts. Shows estimated value, value range, and historical price trends.
- **Property Details:** Full listing info including photos, description, lot size, year built, construction type, heating/cooling, roof type, parking, interior features, and appliances.
- **Tax History:** View historical property tax assessments and amounts.
- **Price History:** See all past sale prices, listing price changes, and status updates for a property.
- **Comparable Sales (Comps):** View recently sold homes nearby with similar characteristics to estimate value.
- **Days on Market:** See how long a property has been listed.
- **Listing Source:** Attribution to the MLS or brokerage providing the listing data.

#### 3D Tours and Visual Tools

- **3D Home Tours:** Walk through a home virtually with interactive 3D tour technology.
- **SkyTour (2025):** Drone-like 3D aerial view of a home's exterior powered by Gaussian splatting rendering technology. Available on Showcase listings.
- **Zillow Showcase Listings:** Premium listings with professional photography, interactive floor plans, AI-powered room restyling, and 3D tours.
- **AI Room Restyling:** On Showcase listings, AI restyles empty rooms in different design styles (modern, Scandinavian, farmhouse, etc.) so buyers can visualize the space.
- **Virtual Staging:** Digitally furnish empty rooms to help buyers envision living in the space.
- **Photo Galleries:** High-resolution photos with room labels and the ability to save favorites.

#### Mortgage and Financial Tools

- **Mortgage Calculator:** Estimate monthly payments with principal, interest, property tax, homeowners insurance, PMI, and HOA fees. Adjust down payment, loan term, and interest rate.
- **BuyAbility:** Real-time personalized estimate of affordable home price and monthly payment based on income, credit score, monthly debts, down payment savings, and preferred monthly payment. Powered by Zillow Home Loans.
- **Offer Insights (2025):** Toggle between different offer price levels to see how competitive each offer might be in the current market. Helps buyers and agents understand offer strength.
- **Verified Pre-Approval:** Guided digital flow to get mortgage pre-approved with secure information verification.
- **Zillow Home Loans:** Direct mortgage lending integrated into the buying experience.
- **Affordability Calculator:** Determine how much home you can afford based on your financial profile.

#### Buying and Touring

- **Tour Itineraries (2025):** Buyers and agents can add homes to a shared tour list, coordinate logistics, and view upcoming/past tours from a centralized tab.
- **Self Home Tours:** Available on Zillow-owned homes. No appointment needed, no sales pressure.
- **Open House Listings:** Browse upcoming open houses on the map or in list view.
- **Tour Requests:** Request in-person tours directly through the app. Connect with listing agents.
- **Favorites and Collections:** Save homes to favorites lists, organize by collection, and share with co-shoppers or partners.
- **Co-Shopping Tools:** Tag favorite features, share favorites, and coordinate searches with a partner or roommate.

#### Selling Features

- **Owner Dashboard:** Claim property ownership, view Zestimate, track home value over time, and manage your listing.
- **For Sale By Owner (FSBO):** List your home for free without an agent. Add photos, description, price, and property details.
- **Agent Finder:** Browse and connect with local real estate agents. View agent ratings, past sales, and specialties.
- **Spruce Dashboard:** Track title and escrow tasks during the closing process in one connected view.
- **Home Value Report:** Detailed breakdown of your home's estimated value and market trends.

#### Rental Features

- **Rental Listings:** Browse apartments, houses, rooms, and condos for rent.
- **Room Listings:** Search for and list individual rooms for rent. Find roommates and share costs.
- **Rental Costs and Fees Breakdown (2025):** Transparent view of all rental costs beyond monthly rent (deposits, pet fees, utilities, parking).
- **Rent Affordability Calculator:** Estimate how much rent you can afford based on income and expenses.
- **Rent Zestimate:** Estimated monthly rent value for properties.
- **Online Applications:** Apply for rentals directly through the platform.
- **Zillow Payments:** Online rent collection via ACH (free) or debit/credit card (small fee).

#### Neighborhood and Location Data

- **Walk Score / Transit Score / Bike Score:** Walkability, transit access, and bikeability ratings for every listing.
- **Travel Times:** See commute times from a listing to important locations (work, school) via driving, transit, biking, or walking.
- **School Data:** Nearby school information including ratings and distance.
- **Neighborhood Overview:** Area demographics, median home value, and local amenities.
- **Climate Risk Data:** Previously showed flood, fire, heat, wind, and air-quality scores on listings (removed late 2025 after controversy; now links to First Street's site for lookup).

#### Notifications and Alerts

- **Price Drop Alerts:** Get notified when saved homes drop in price.
- **New Listing Alerts:** Instant, daily, or weekly emails when new homes match saved search criteria.
- **Status Change Alerts:** Notifications when a saved listing changes status (pending, sold, back on market).
- **Open House Reminders:** Alerts for upcoming open houses on saved listings.

#### Free (all features are free for consumers)

### Homes.com (CoStar Group)

**Company:** CoStar Group | **Monthly Users:** 108M (fastest-growing portal) | **Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web

- **Homes AI (2026):** Full conversational AI assistant powered by Microsoft Azure OpenAI. Real-time two-way voice or text conversations to search, refine, explore, defurnish rooms, discover neighborhoods, and answer questions without leaving the page.

- **Filtering:** Filter by price, bedrooms, bathrooms, home type, lot size, year built, and more.

- **Foreclosure Listings:** Browse bank-owned and foreclosure properties.
- **New Construction Search:** Dedicated search for new builds. Explore builders, communities, floor plans, and pricing.
- **Open House Search:** Find open houses with real-time updates. Connect directly with hosting agents.
- **Homes.com Boost:** Paid feature for sellers to increase listing visibility and reach more buyers.

#### 3D Tours and Visual Tools

- **Matterport 3D Virtual Tours:** Immersive 3D digital twin walkthroughs of listed properties. Walk through rooms, view floor plans, revisit spaces, and share tours with family and friends. Complimentary capture in eligible locations.
- **AI Room Defurnishing:** Homes AI can instantly remove existing furniture from a Matterport 3D tour, turning any room into a blank canvas for visualization.
- **AI-Guided Home Tours:** Homes AI transforms Matterport tours into immersive guided experiences, narrating and answering questions as you walk through.
- **AI Spatial Measurements:** Homes AI delivers precise room measurements within the 3D tour for accurate space planning.

#### Neighborhood and Schools Data

- **Neighborhood Research:** Video tours, in-depth research, and articles covering 20,000+ neighborhoods.
- **Neighborhood Insights:** Data on demographics, local amenities, dining, shopping, parks, and community character.
- **School Data:** Detailed school overviews with photos, videos, rankings from multiple trusted sources, and the ability to compare and cross-reference school information.
- **Market Intelligence:** Proprietary CoStar data on local market conditions, inventory levels, and price trends.
- **Monthly Market Reports:** Analytics team-produced reports on inventory levels and price changes.

#### Agent Connection

- **Agent Profiles:** Search over 1 million agent profiles with bios, deal history, areas of specialty, past sales, and client reviews.
- **Agent Video Bios:** Agents create video introductions so buyers and sellers can get to know them before connecting.
- **Direct Agent Connection:** Connect directly with listing agents or buyer's agents through the platform. No intermediary lead routing.
- **Agent Match:** Find experienced agents specializing in your target neighborhoods and home types.

#### Content and Education

- **Real Estate News:** Dedicated editorial team covering mortgage updates, buying/selling trends, and market developments.
- **Learning Center:** Articles and guides on the home buying and selling process, mortgage basics, and real estate comps.
- **Real Estate Comps Tool:** Understand comparable sales to evaluate home value.

#### Data Security

- **Proprietary Data Ecosystem:** All Homes AI data stays within Homes.com's proprietary systems. Never used to train or refine external AI models.

## VEHICLE MAINTENANCE APPS

### Drivvo

**Company:** Drivvo (MWM) | **Downloads:** 10M+ (Google Play) | **Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web

#### Fuel Tracking

- **Fuel Log:** Record every fill-up with date, odometer reading, fuel type, volume, price per unit, total cost, and gas station. Supports full tank and partial fill logging.
- **Fuel Efficiency Calculation:** Automatically calculates consumption in multiple unit formats: MPG, gallon/100mi, mi/L, km/gallon, L/100km, km/L. Tracks best and worst efficiency readings over time.
- **Cost Per Mile/Kilometer:** Calculate and track running fuel cost per distance traveled.
- **Multi-Fuel Support:** Track gasoline, ethanol, diesel, LPG (propane), CNG (compressed natural gas), and electric charging.
- **Flex Calculator:** Compare cost-effectiveness between gasoline and ethanol to determine which fuel gives the better deal (particularly useful in Brazil/flex-fuel markets).
- **Fuel Price Tracking:** Track fuel prices over time and by gas station location.

#### Maintenance and Service Tracking

- **Maintenance Log (Preventive):** Record scheduled preventive maintenance such as oil changes, filter replacements, tire rotations, brake inspections, and fluid top-offs. Track date, cost, odometer, and service provider.
- **Service Log (Corrective):** Track unplanned repairs and corrective services with full detail.
- **Service Categories:** Pre-built categories for common services (oil change, brake check, tire replacement, AC cleaning, timing belt, etc.) with the ability to create custom service categories.
- **Service Reminders:** Set reminders based on time intervals (e.g., every 6 months) or mileage intervals (e.g., every 5,000 miles). Receive push notifications when services are due.

#### Expense Tracking

- **General Expense Log:** Record any vehicle-related expense: insurance premiums, registration fees, parking, tolls, car washes, fines, taxes, and accessories. Create custom expense categories.
- **Income Tracking:** Record vehicle-related income for professional drivers, rideshare operators, or fleet managers. Track earnings vs. expenses for profitability analysis.
- **Category Management:** Create and customize expense categories to match your needs.

#### Route and Trip Tracking

- **Route Logging:** Record trips with start/end locations, distance, purpose (personal vs. business), and notes. Useful for mileage reports, tax deductions, and business expense documentation.
- **Business Trip Separation:** Log business trips separately for export and reimbursement.

#### Checklists

- **Pre-Trip Checklists:** Create and use vehicle inspection checklists (tire pressure, fluid levels, lights, brakes) before trips or on a regular schedule.

#### Reports and Analytics

- **Charts and Graphs:** Visual reports showing fuel consumption trends, expense breakdowns by category, cost comparisons over time, and vehicle-by-vehicle analysis.
- **Date-Range Filtering:** View reports for specific time periods.
- **Module-Based Reports:** Separate reporting for refueling, maintenance, expenses, income, and routes.
- **Fleet Comparison:** Compare usage and costs across multiple vehicles.

#### Multi-Vehicle and Fleet Management

- **Multiple Vehicles:** Manage an unlimited number of vehicles within a single account. Each vehicle has its own independent history and statistics.
- **Vehicle Profiles:** Store make, model, year, VIN, license plate, and photo for each vehicle.
- **Fleet Dashboard:** Overview of all vehicles with key metrics.

#### Free vs. Premium (Drivvo Pro)

**Free:**
- All core tracking features (fuel, maintenance, expenses, routes, checklists, reminders)
- Basic reports and charts
- Single-device usage
- Ad-supported

**Premium (Drivvo Pro):**
- Cloud backup and data synchronization across devices
- Ad-free experience
- CSV/Excel data export
- Priority support

### Jerry

**Company:** Jerry, Inc. | **Funding:** $242M | **Users:** 5M+ | **Platforms:** iOS, Android

#### PriceProtect (Insurance Comparison and Switching)

- **Multi-Carrier Quote Comparison:** Compare real-time quotes from 55+ top car insurance carriers in under 2 minutes. AI-powered matching based on driver profile.
- **One-Tap Switching:** Switch insurance carriers directly through the app. Jerry handles cancellation of old policy and activation of new policy.
- **Average Savings Tracking:** Drivers who switch save an average of $600/year. The app displays personal savings potential.
- **Home Insurance Comparison:** Compare and purchase home insurance from multiple carriers.
- **Renters Insurance Comparison:** Compare and purchase renters insurance.
- **Bundle Discounts:** Identify and apply multi-policy bundle discounts (auto + home, auto + renters).
- **Continuous Monitoring:** Jerry monitors rates and notifies you when a better deal is available, even after you have already switched.
- **Policy Management:** View current policy details, coverage levels, deductibles, and payment schedule in-app.

#### GarageGuard (Maintenance and Repairs)

- **Automated Maintenance Schedule:** Tracks your car's maintenance needs based on previous service dates, manufacturer-recommended service intervals, and average miles driven. Alerts you when service is due.
- **Car Diagnostics:** Symptom-based diagnostic tool ("WebMD for your car"). Describe symptoms and get likely problems, solutions, and urgency level.
- **Repair Cost Estimates:** Location-specific estimates for parts, labor, and completion time tailored to your vehicle's make, model, and year.
- **Shop Finder:** Locate trusted local repair shops with ratings, price estimates, and distance. Filter by service type.
- **Recall Alerts:** Automatic notifications when your vehicle has a manufacturer safety recall. Includes recall details and next steps.
- **Digital Glovebox:** Secure digital storage for service receipts, driver's license, proof of insurance, registration, and other vehicle documents.
- **Service History Tracking:** Maintain a running log of all maintenance and repairs performed.

#### DriveShield (Driving Safety and Rewards)

- **Driving Score:** Phone sensors monitor driving behavior (acceleration, braking, speed, cornering, phone usage) and generate a free driving score per trip and overall.
- **Trip Review:** View safety metrics for each individual trip. Review problem areas and improvement trends.
- **Mileage Logging:** Automatic mileage tracking per trip for business expense reports and tax deductions.
- **Safe Driving Rewards:** Each safe trip earns points redeemable at 50+ partners including Home Chef, Goodyear, and AMC Theaters.
- **Location Sharing:** Share real-time location with family and friends. Set arrival/departure location alerts.
- **Family Leaderboard:** Compare driving scores with family members.
- **Parking Location Memory:** Automatically detects and saves your parking spot location and time so you can find your car later.
- **Driver vs. Passenger Detection:** Distinguishes between trips where you are driving vs. riding as a passenger. Manual correction available for misidentified trips.
- **Instant Safety Feedback:** Real-time tips and feedback after each drive with suggestions for safer driving habits.

#### DataLock (Privacy and Security)

- **DataLock Guarantee:** When shopping for insurance through Jerry, your data is never shared with third parties. Zero spam calls guaranteed.
- **Bank-Level Encryption:** All personal and financial data encrypted in transit and at rest.
- **User-Controlled Data Sharing:** Driving data from DriveShield is only shared with insurers if you explicitly opt in. Full opt-out at any time.

#### Car Buying and Financing

- **Car Loan Comparison:** Compare auto loan offers from multiple lenders.
- **Refinancing:** Find lower-rate auto loans to reduce monthly payments on existing car loans.
- **Insurance Research by Vehicle:** Look up average insurance costs for specific makes and models before purchasing.

#### Support

- **24/7 In-App Chat:** Live support via chat.
- **Text Support:** Reach Jerry agents via text message.
- **Phone Support:** Mon-Fri 8am-midnight ET, Sat-Sun 8am-6:30pm ET.
- **Email Support:** Asynchronous support channel.

#### Free (all features are free for consumers; Jerry earns commissions from insurance carriers)

#### Maintenance Reminders and Alerts

- **Customized Service Alerts:** Know when your car is due for oil changes, tire rotations, filter replacements, brake inspections, fluid flushes, and state inspections. Based on manufacturer recommendations and your driving patterns.
- **Registration Renewal Reminders:** Alerts when your vehicle registration is approaching its renewal date.
- **Safety Recall Alerts:** Automatic notifications when a manufacturer safety recall is issued for your vehicle's make, model, and year. Includes recall details and recommended action.
- **"Do Not Drive" Recall Warnings:** Urgent alerts for serious recalls where the vehicle should not be driven until repaired.

#### Vehicle Value and Cost Tools

- **History-Based Value Estimate:** View your car's current estimated market value based on its service history, mileage, age, and condition. Updated regularly.
- **Repair Cost Estimates:** See expected price ranges for common maintenance and repair jobs in your area.
- **Fuel Efficiency Tracking:** Update odometer readings and track fuel economy (MPG or L/100km) with charts and logs over time.

#### Shop Finder

- **Verified Shop Ratings:** Browse auto repair shops in your area with verified ratings and reviews from real customers.
- **CARFAX Network Shops:** Shops in the CARFAX network automatically report service records to your vehicle history.
- **Service Specialty Filtering:** Find shops specializing in your service need (brakes, transmission, tires, etc.).

#### Multi-Vehicle Management

- **Up to 8 Vehicles:** Track maintenance, recalls, and service history for up to 8 cars in a single account.
- **Vehicle Personalization:** Add a photo and nickname for each vehicle for easy identification.
- **License Plate Recognition:** Add vehicles by scanning your license plate. Automatically loads vehicle information.
- **VIN Lookup:** Add vehicles via VIN for precise make/model identification.

#### Dashboard

- **At-a-Glance Dashboard:** Customized view showing each vehicle's upcoming service needs, open recalls, estimated value, and recent service history.
- **Odometer Tracking:** Regularly update mileage to keep service schedules accurate.

## PLANT CARE APPS

### Planta

**Company:** Planta (Stromming AB) | **Users:** 7M | **Plants Tracked:** 32M+ | **Platforms:** iOS, Android

- **AI Plant Scanner:** Take a photo of any plant and Planta identifies the species instantly, providing common name, scientific name, and care requirements. (Premium)

- **Barcode/Tag Scanning:** Scan nursery tags or barcodes to add plants with pre-loaded care data.

#### Watering and Care Reminders

- **Intelligent Watering Reminders:** AI-powered watering schedule using 30+ parameters including plant species, pot size, soil type, room placement, and local climate/weather data. Adjusts dynamically as conditions change.
- **Fertilizing Reminders:** Scheduled reminders for when to fertilize each plant, with product and dosage guidance. (Premium)
- **Misting Reminders:** Notifications for humidity-loving plants that need regular misting. (Premium)
- **Repotting Reminders:** Alerts when a plant has outgrown its pot, with guidance on pot size and soil mix. (Premium)
- **Cleaning Reminders:** Reminders to clean dust from leaves for optimal photosynthesis. (Premium)
- **Pruning Reminders:** Scheduled pruning notifications with technique guidance. (Premium)
- **Overwintering Instructions:** Seasonal care adjustments for plants that need different winter care (reduced watering, temperature changes, dormancy). (Premium)
- **Adaptive Scheduling:** Care schedules automatically adjust based on season, weather changes, and plant feedback.

#### Plant Health and Diagnosis

- **Dr. Planta:** AI-powered plant health diagnosis. Take a photo of a sick plant and get diagnosis of the problem (yellowing leaves, pests, root rot, overwatering, etc.) with a step-by-step treatment plan. (Premium)
- **Expert Support:** Premium subscribers can email Planta's in-house plant experts with photos and questions for personalized advice via contact@getplanta.com or the in-app form. (Premium)

#### Room and Light Management

- **Light Meter:** Use your phone's camera to measure real-time light levels in any room. Categorizes light as low, medium, bright indirect, or direct sun. (Premium)
- **Room Assignment:** Assign plants to specific rooms. The app uses room light data to tailor care schedules.
- **Plant Placement Recommendations:** Get suggestions for which plants will thrive in specific rooms based on measured light conditions. (Premium)
- **Personalized Plant Recommendations:** AI recommends new plants based on your skill level, home environment, light conditions, and existing collection. (Premium)

#### Plant Organization and Journaling

- **Plant Library:** Organize all your plants in a visual library with photos, names, and locations.
- **Plant Journal:** Document plant growth with dated photos, notes, and milestones. Track care history over time. (Premium)
- **Care History Log:** View a timeline of all care actions performed on each plant (watered, fertilized, repotted, etc.).
- **Custom Nicknames:** Give each plant a personal name.

#### Community and Sharing

- **Care Share:** Share your Planta care schedule with family members or friends. Multiple people can coordinate care tasks in real time, seeing when tasks are completed. (Premium)
- **Community Discussions:** Engage with other plant enthusiasts in the Planta community. Share tips, ask questions, and celebrate plant successes.

#### Care Guides and Education

- **Step-by-Step Care Guides:** Detailed articles on watering technique, light requirements, soil preferences, common pests, propagation, and seasonal care for thousands of species.
- **Skill-Level Matching:** App adapts guidance based on user skill level, from "Hopeless" beginner to "Collector" and "Jungle Owner."
- **Indoor and Outdoor Support:** Care guidance for both houseplants and outdoor garden plants.
