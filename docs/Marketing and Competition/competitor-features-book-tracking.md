# Competitor Features: Book Tracking Apps

Comprehensive feature inventory of the five leading book tracking applications, compiled February 2026.

---

## 1. Goodreads

**Overview:** The dominant social book cataloging platform with 150M+ members, owned by Amazon since 2013. Web, iOS, and Android. Free with ads.

### Library Management

- **Default shelves:** Read, Currently Reading, Want to Read -- every user starts with these three exclusive shelves
- **Custom shelves:** Create unlimited user-defined shelves (e.g., "favorites," "abandoned," genre-specific)
- **Exclusive vs. non-exclusive shelves:** Default shelves are exclusive (a book can only be on one); custom shelves can overlap
- **Multiple editions:** View and switch between different editions of the same book (hardcover, paperback, audiobook, etc.)
- **Series tracking:** Browse complete series listings, see which installments you have read, and navigate to the next entry
- **Barcode scanner:** Scan book covers or barcodes in a bookstore to instantly look up a title
- **Manual book entry:** Add books not found in the database by entering details manually
- **ISBN search:** Look up books by ISBN
- **Reading progress:** Log page number or percentage completion and share progress updates
- **Date tracking:** Record date started and date finished for each book
- **Re-reads:** Mark a book as re-read and log multiple reading instances
- **Book details:** View publication info, page count, original title, language, literary awards, and linked editions

### Ratings and Reviews

- **Star ratings:** Rate books on a 1-5 star scale (whole stars only, no half-star support)
- **Written reviews:** Compose long-form reviews with optional spoiler tags
- **Review drafts:** Auto-save draft reviews while composing
- **Review likes:** Other users can "like" your reviews
- **Review comments:** Comment on other users' reviews
- **Review sorting:** Sort reviews by recency, popularity, or star rating
- **Community ratings distribution:** View the histogram of all ratings for a title

### Social Features

- **Friends and followers:** Add friends, follow other readers, view their shelves and activity
- **News feed:** See book reviews, ratings, progress updates, and status updates from friends
- **Feed customization:** Filter and control what types of updates appear in your feed
- **Friend suggestions:** Receive suggestions based on mutual friends and shared reading tastes
- **Messaging:** Send private messages to other Goodreads users
- **Recommend books:** Suggest specific titles to friends
- **Compare books:** Compare your reading history and ratings against another user
- **Reading updates:** Post status updates about what you are reading, with optional page references

### Discovery and Recommendations

- **Personalized recommendations:** Algorithm analyzes 20 billion data points to suggest titles based on reading history and ratings
- **Genre browsing:** Explore 29+ genre categories (Art, Biography, Fantasy, Romance, Science Fiction, etc.)
- **New releases:** View recently published books and upcoming titles
- **Popular/trending lists:** See what is trending on the platform
- **Book lists:** Browse thousands of user-created and editorially curated lists (e.g., "Best Sci-Fi of 2025")
- **Listopia:** Vote on and contribute to ranked book lists
- **Similar books:** Each book page shows reader-suggested "Readers also enjoyed" titles

### Community Features

- **Groups:** Join discussion groups organized by genre, theme, or topic
- **Book clubs:** Create or join online book clubs
- **Q&A / Ask the Author:** Ask authors questions directly from their profile
- **Quotes:** Browse and contribute a massive library of book quotes; search quotes by keyword or theme
- **Trivia and quizzes:** Community-created literary trivia
- **Discussion forums:** Participate in topic threads within groups

### Reading Challenge

- **Annual reading challenge:** Set a goal for the number of books to read each year
- **Progress tracker:** Visual progress bar showing books read vs. goal
- **Friends' challenge progress:** View how friends are progressing on their own challenges
- **Year in Books:** Annual summary page showing all books read that year with stats

### Author Features

- **Author program:** Published authors can claim profiles, update bios, connect blog RSS feeds, and engage readers
- **Author dashboard:** Analytics on book views, ratings, and reviews
- **Author Q&A:** Readers can submit questions; authors respond publicly
- **Author blog integration:** RSS feed from an author's blog posts directly on their Goodreads profile
- **Author events:** List and promote upcoming author appearances and book signings

### Giveaways

- **Book giveaways:** Publishers and authors run giveaways of physical and digital books
- **Giveaway entry:** Users enter with one click; winners are selected and notified
- **Giveaway browsing:** Filter giveaways by genre, format, and end date

### Goodreads Choice Awards

- **Annual awards:** Community-voted awards across 20+ categories
- **Nomination rounds:** Multiple voting rounds narrow down finalists
- **Winner announcements:** Browse current and historical winners

### Import/Export

- **CSV export:** Download entire reading history (titles, ratings, shelves, dates, reviews) as a CSV file
- **Import from other platforms:** Upload CSV files to import reading data
- **API (legacy):** Historically offered a developer API; API key issuance was discontinued in December 2020

### Kindle Integration

- **Kindle sync:** Books read on Kindle can automatically appear on Goodreads shelves
- **Kindle highlights and notes:** Share highlights and quotes from Kindle to Goodreads (via Kindle Paperwhite and Kindle apps)
- **Friends' updates in Kindle:** View Goodreads friends' activity directly within the Kindle iOS app

### Notifications and Settings

- **Email notifications:** Configurable alerts for friend requests, recommendations, group activity, author posts, and giveaways
- **Push notifications:** Mobile alerts for social interactions and updates
- **Privacy settings:** Control who can see your shelves, reviews, and profile information
- **Account settings:** Manage email, password, connected accounts, and data export

### Platform and Accessibility

- **Web app:** Full-featured browser experience
- **iOS app:** Native iPhone/iPad app
- **Android app:** Native Android app
- **Ads:** Free-tier includes advertising across web and mobile

## 2. The StoryGraph

**Overview:** Independent, Black and woman-owned Goodreads alternative with 4M+ signups. Known for mood-based recommendations, rich statistics, and content warnings. Web, iOS, and Android. Free with optional StoryGraph Plus ($4.99/mo or $49.99/yr).

### Library Management

- **Shelves:** Read, Currently Reading, Want to Read, Did Not Finish (DNF built-in)
- **To-Read Pile:** Queue system allowing you to prioritize up to 5 books from your Want to Read shelf
- **Custom tags:** Create user-defined tags to organize books beyond default shelves
- **Owned books tracking:** Mark which books you physically or digitally own
- **Favorites shelf:** Add books to a Favorites list and pin 5 to display on your profile
- **Pause books:** Officially pause a book; paused periods are excluded from reading time calculations
- **Book formats:** Track print, digital, and audiobook formats separately
- **Barcode scanner:** Scan book barcodes to search and add books
- **Single edition view:** Toggle between combined edition views and specific editions
- **Reading journal:** Activity log capturing all reading updates over time

### Ratings and Reviews

- **Granular star ratings:** Rate books with quarter-star and half-star precision (e.g., 3.25, 4.5)
- **Written reviews:** Post reviews that can include mood, pace, and content tags
- **Community review summaries:** AI-generated summaries of what the community thinks about a book, including character assessments
- **Live reactions:** Add reactions to specific parts of a book without spoiling for others
- **DNF tracking:** Mark books as Did Not Finish with page number where you stopped

### Discovery and Recommendations

- **Mood-based discovery:** Filter books by mood (adventurous, funny, dark, emotional, hopeful, informative, inspiring, lighthearted, mysterious, reflective, sad, tense)
- **Pace filtering:** Filter by reading pace (fast, medium, slow)
- **Advanced filters:** Filter by genre, page count, publication year, fiction/nonfiction, format, tags
- **AI-powered recommendations:** Machine learning engine that learns from your reading history and preferences
- **Similar books:** Browse books similar to any title
- **Similar users:** Discover readers with overlapping taste
- **Popular this week:** See what the StoryGraph community is reading now
- **Browse and explore:** General discovery interface for exploring the catalog

### Content Warnings

- **User-submitted content warnings:** Community flags content types at three severity levels (graphic, moderate, minor)
- **Author-approved content warnings:** Authors can add official warnings to their books
- **Flagged content alerts:** Set topics you want to be warned about; warning icons appear on book pages that match
- **Content type filtering:** Specify topics, themes, and tropes you prefer to avoid; filter results accordingly

### Statistics and Analytics

- **Reading goal tracking:** Set goals by number of books, pages, or minutes/hours (audiobook-friendly)
- **Monthly wrap-up graphics:** Shareable visual summaries of monthly reading activity
- **Annual statistics:** Comprehensive year-end stats including:
  - Total books and pages read
  - Mood breakdown (pie chart)
  - Pace analysis
  - Page count distribution
  - Audiobook length tracking
  - Fiction vs. nonfiction split
  - Genre distribution
  - Most-read authors
  - Language variety
  - Star rating distribution
  - Format comparison (print, digital, audiobook)
  - Reading timeline graphs
- **Year-over-year comparison:** Compare stats across multiple years (Plus feature)
- **Custom charts:** Create custom pie and bar charts from your reading data (Plus feature)
- **Custom stats dashboard:** Build a personalized stats view (Plus feature)
- **Filtered statistics:** Slice stats by timeframe, fiction/nonfiction, moods, genres (Plus feature)

### Social and Community Features

- **Profile options:** Public, private, or community-visible profiles
- **Friend system:** Customizable follow settings (follow-back, approval required, open)
- **Community page:** Aggregated reading updates from people you follow
- **Similar users:** Discover readers with comparable taste profiles

### Group Reading

- **Buddy reads:** Read a book with up to 8 participants; create section-based discussion forums
- **Readalongs / Book clubs:** Read in groups of up to 1,000 participants with checkpoints and section forums
- **Spoiler-safe forums:** Section forums can be locked until readers reach that part of the book
- **Customizable logistics:** Set descriptions, optional end dates, and discussion structure
- **Suggested buddy read books:** Plus users can get AI-suggested titles for group reads

### Reading Challenges

- **Annual reading challenge:** Set a yearly goal for book count
- **Page/minute goals:** Alternative challenge types based on pages or listening time
- **Prompt challenges:** Thematic challenges with specific reading prompts (e.g., "read a book set in another country")
- **Challenge progress indicators:** Visual tracking of how far you are toward completing each challenge

### Giveaways

- **Community giveaways:** Enter to win books
- **Giveaway filtering:** Filter by mood, pace, type, genre, page count, format, and location
- **Author/publisher submissions:** Authors and publishers can submit giveaway listings

### Profile and Personalization

- **Profile customization:** Username, photo, pronouns, social media links, custom bio (160 characters)
- **Reading habits description:** Describe your reading preferences on your profile
- **Currently reading display:** Show what you are reading now
- **5-star books showcase:** Highlight your top-rated reads
- **Tag display:** Show your custom tags on your profile
- **Reading challenge visibility:** Toggle whether your challenge progress is public
- **Reading preference survey:** Take a survey to fine-tune recommendation accuracy

### Import/Export and Account

- **Goodreads import:** Bulk import shelves, ratings, and dates from Goodreads CSV export
- **Custom shelf mapping:** Map Goodreads custom shelves to StoryGraph tags during import
- **Theme selection:** System default, light mode, or dark mode
- **Timezone settings:** Set your local timezone for accurate date tracking
- **Language preferences:** Choose interface language
- **Audiobook format preferences:** Configure how audiobook time is tracked and displayed
- **Notification management:** Control email and push notification categories
- **Data management:** Delete specific shelves or entire account

### StoryGraph Plus (Premium)

- **Custom chart creation:** Build pie and bar charts from your reading data
- **Advanced stats filtering:** Filter stats by multiple dimensions simultaneously
- **Multi-timeframe comparisons:** Compare stats across custom date ranges
- **Year-over-year comparison charts:** Visual trend analysis across years
- **Suggested buddy read books:** AI suggestions for group reads
- **Roadmap voting and commenting:** Influence platform development priorities
- **Priority customer support:** Faster response times
- **Pricing:** $4.99/month or $49.99/year, with a 30-day free trial

### Platform

- **Web app:** Full-featured browser experience
- **iOS app:** Native iPhone app
- **Android app:** Native Android app
- **Changelog:** Public changelog documenting all platform updates
- **Public roadmap:** Transparent feature roadmap with community voting

## 3. Bookly

**Overview:** Mobile-first reading session tracker focused on building reading habits through gamification and real-time session timing. 500K+ Android downloads. iOS and Android. Free (limited to 10 books) with Bookly Pro ($4.99/mo, $19.99/6mo, $29.99/yr, 7-day free trial).

### Reading Timer and Sessions

- **Real-time reading timer:** Tap to start/stop a reading session; tracks time spent reading
- **Page number logging:** Record your current page at the start and end of each session
- **Reading speed calculation:** Computes pages per minute/hour based on session data
- **Estimated finish time:** Predicts when you will finish a book based on your current reading pace
- **Countdown timer:** Set a target session duration and count down
- **Session pause/resume:** Pause a reading session without losing progress
- **Ambient sounds:** Play background sounds during reading sessions (e.g., Light Rain, Fireplace, Coffee Shop); more options with Pro
- **In-session thought logging:** Jot down thoughts as they occur during a reading session
- **In-session quote capture:** Photograph, type, or voice-dictate a favorite quote mid-session
- **In-session vocabulary:** Note words to look up later during a reading session

### Library Management

- **Book collection:** Add books via barcode scanner or online database search
- **Collection limit:** Free users limited to 10 books; unlimited with Pro
- **Custom collections:** Organize books into collections by year, genre, or custom criteria (Pro)
- **Book formats:** Track physical books, ebooks, and audiobooks
- **Lent/borrowed tracking:** Mark books as lent out or borrowed
- **Manual book entry:** Add books not found in the database manually
- **Grid view:** Display library in a visual grid layout
- **Book deletion:** Remove books from collection (Pro feature for bulk management)
- **Goodreads sync:** Synchronize library with Goodreads account
- **Goodreads import:** Import reading history from Goodreads

### Ratings and Reviews

- **Star ratings:** Rate books with half-star precision
- **Custom rating categories:** Rate on additional dimensions -- inspiration, heartfelt, love, spice, humor
- **Written reviews:** Add personal thoughts and reflections per book
- **Rate without finishing:** Option to rate a book you did not complete

### Quotes and Notes

- **Quote saving:** Save favorite passages via photo, typing, or voice dictation
- **Image quotes:** Create shareable image cards from saved quotes (Pro)
- **Digital annotations:** Add notes tied to specific books
- **Voice notes:** Record spoken thoughts using speech-to-text
- **Photo capture:** Snap pictures of important passages
- **Lock screen quote display:** Show a rotating quote on your device lock screen

### Character Tracking

- **Character management:** Add characters to books to remember cast members
- **Character descriptions:** Store notes about each character
- **Character evolution:** Document how characters change throughout the story
- **Character relationships:** Map connections between characters
- **Challenge tracking:** Track character-related reading challenges

### Statistics and Reports

- **Total read time:** Lifetime and per-period reading time
- **Pages read counter:** Total pages across all books
- **Reading speed metrics:** Average pages per minute/hour, fastest and slowest pace
- **Daily reading time:** Time spent reading each day
- **Reading streaks:** Consecutive days of reading activity
- **Book type breakdown:** Percentage stats by format or genre
- **Most popular genres:** Identify your most-read genres
- **Infographic reports:** Visual summaries per book, per month, and per year with graphs
- **PDF summaries:** Generate PDF summary documents for completed books (Pro)
- **Global stats:** See aggregated community statistics (Pro)

### Goals and Motivation

- **Daily reading goals:** Set a target for daily reading time, pages, or books
- **Monthly reading goals:** Monthly targets for time, pages, or books
- **Yearly reading goals:** Annual targets for time, pages, or books
- **Daily reminders:** Configurable push notifications to remind you to read
- **Achievement badges:** Unlock badges for hitting milestones (e.g., first book finished, 7-day streak)
- **Readathon participation:** Join organized Bookly Readathon events with community progress tracking
- **Readathon prompt tracking:** Follow themed prompts during readathon events

### Gamification

- **Diamond currency:** Earn diamonds based on reading session duration
- **Character customization:** Spend diamonds to unlock costumes for Bloo (the app mascot)
- **Seasonal/holiday costumes:** Special themed outfits for Bloo
- **App icon customization:** Spend diamonds to change your home screen app icon
- **Level-up system:** Progress through levels based on reading activity
- **Streak repair:** Use diamonds to repair a broken reading streak

### Personal Reading Assistant

- **Bloo assistant:** AI-powered personal reading assistant showing stats summaries, ratings, current level, goals, and readathon progress (Pro)
- **Personalized reading suggestions:** AI-driven book recommendations based on your reading history (Pro)

### Social and Sharing

- **Progress sharing:** Share current reading progress from within the app
- **Infographic sharing:** Share visual reading stats to social media
- **Community feature:** See how many other Bookly users are reading right now
- **Calendar sharing:** Share your reading calendar (with option to hide streak repairs)

### Widgets and System Integration

- **Home screen widgets:** Display reading stats, current book, or streak on home screen
- **Lock screen widgets:** Reading stats on lock screen (iOS)
- **Control Center integration:** Quick actions from iOS Control Center
- **Dynamic Island:** Live activity reading timer on iPhone 14 Pro and later
- **iCloud sync:** Sync reading data across Apple devices
- **Cloud sync:** Cross-device sync (Pro)

### Accessibility

- **Dark mode:** Full dark mode interface
- **Large text support:** Scale text up to 200%
- **Enhanced contrast:** High contrast option
- **Color differentiation:** Alternative color schemes for colorblind users

### Bookly Pro (Premium)

- **Unlimited books:** No 10-book collection cap
- **All collections features:** Organize by year, genre, custom criteria
- **Cloud sync:** Cross-device synchronization
- **Extended ambient sounds:** Additional background sound options
- **Image quotes:** Create shareable quote cards
- **Global stats:** Community-wide statistics
- **Bloo assistant:** AI reading assistant
- **Personalized suggestions:** AI book recommendations
- **PDF summaries:** Generated book summaries
- **Ad-free experience:** Remove all advertisements
- **Pricing:** $4.99/month, $19.99/6 months, $29.99/year; 7-day free trial

## 4. Libby

**Overview:** Free library lending app by OverDrive, providing access to ebooks, audiobooks, and magazines from public libraries. 9.1M+ installs. No subscription fees, no in-app purchases, no late fees. iOS, Android, Fire tablets, and web.

### Content Access

- **Ebooks:** Borrow ebooks from your public library's digital collection
- **Audiobooks:** Borrow digital audiobooks
- **Magazines:** Browse and check out 3,000+ magazines (do not count toward borrow limit)
- **Read-along narrated ebooks:** Follow along with professionally narrated ebooks that highlight text as it is read
- **Free access:** Completely free -- no subscription, no in-app purchases, no late fees
- **Library card integration:** Connect one or more library cards from different library systems
- **Multi-library support:** Browse and borrow from multiple library systems simultaneously

### Borrowing and Holds

- **One-tap borrowing:** Borrow available titles instantly
- **Hold system:** Place holds on checked-out titles; receive notification when available
- **Hold queue position:** View your place in line for held titles
- **Automatic returns:** Titles are automatically returned on their due date
- **Early return:** Return titles before the due date if desired
- **Loan management:** View all active loans in one place
- **Loan period:** Library-configured lending periods (typically 7, 14, or 21 days)
- **Renewals:** Renew eligible titles before they expire

### Reading Experience (Ebooks)

- **Built-in ebook reader:** Read ebooks directly within the app
- **Font customization:** Choose from multiple font families
- **Font size adjustment:** Scale text size including extended accessibility sizes
- **OpenDyslexic font:** Dedicated dyslexia-friendly font option with weighted letter bottoms
- **Lighting modes:** Light, dark, and sepia reading backgrounds
- **Book layout customization:** Adjust margins, line spacing, and text alignment
- **Bookmarks:** Add bookmarks to any page
- **Notes:** Write notes tied to specific passages
- **Highlights:** Highlight text in multiple colors
- **Persistent annotations:** Bookmarks, notes, and highlights reappear if you borrow the same book again
- **Offline reading:** Download ebooks for reading without an internet connection
- **Streaming:** Stream ebooks over Wi-Fi or mobile data to save device storage

### Audiobook Features

- **Built-in audio player:** Listen to audiobooks directly within the app
- **Playback speed control:** Adjust speed from 0.6x to 3.0x
- **Sleep timer:** Set a timer to automatically stop playback
- **Bookmarks in audiobooks:** Add bookmarks at specific timestamps
- **Notes in audiobooks:** Attach notes to audiobook positions
- **Offline listening:** Download audiobooks for offline playback
- **Streaming:** Stream audiobooks over Wi-Fi or mobile data
- **Car integration:** Listen via Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, Android Automotive, or Bluetooth
- **Background playback:** Continue listening while using other apps

### Organization and Discovery

- **Tags:** Create custom tags to organize titles into personal lists (e.g., "Book Club Picks," "Summer Reading")
- **Smart tags:** Tags with built-in logic (e.g., auto-sync wishlists from OverDrive)
- **Magazine notifications:** Set up smart tags to be notified when new magazine issues are available
- **Explore/browse:** Browse library catalog by genre, subject, audience, and format
- **Search:** Search across all connected library catalogs
- **"Inspire Me" AI discovery:** AI-powered recommendation feature that suggests books based on prompts, reading history, and preferences (fiction/nonfiction, age range, content type)
- **Curated collections:** Library-curated reading lists and featured collections
- **Availability filtering:** Filter search results to show only currently available titles
- **Timeline:** View your complete borrowing history

### Kindle Integration (U.S. Only)

- **Send to Kindle:** Send borrowed library ebooks to your Kindle device or Kindle app
- **Kindle sync:** Book appears across all Kindle apps and devices tied to your Amazon account
- **Kindle reading:** Read library books using the Kindle reading experience instead of Libby's reader

### Synchronization

- **Cross-device sync:** Loans, notes, bookmarks, and reading progress sync across all devices
- **Seamless handoff:** Pick up reading on any device exactly where you left off

### Accessibility

- **Screen reader support:** Compatible with VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android)
- **"Read From Here" mode:** Optimized reading mode for screen readers
- **OpenDyslexic font:** Dyslexia-friendly font throughout the ebook reader
- **Adjustable text sizes:** Extended range including larger accessibility sizes
- **Keyboard shortcuts:** Navigate, page forward/backward, start/stop playback, adjust settings, and operate sleep timer via keyboard
- **Light/dark mode:** Follows device system setting or can be set manually

### Platform Support

- **iOS app:** iPhone and iPad (iOS 10+)
- **Android app:** Android 7.1+
- **Fire tablets:** Amazon Fire tablet support
- **Web app:** Browser-based reading at libbyapp.com (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- **Windows/Mac/Chromebook:** Access via web browser

## Key Competitive Insights

**Goodreads** dominates through network effects (150M members) and Amazon/Kindle integration, but its product has stagnated (no half-star ratings, aging UI, discontinued API, limited stats).

**StoryGraph** is the most feature-complete alternative, differentiating on mood-based discovery, granular ratings, rich statistics, content warnings, and indie ownership. Its Plus tier adds powerful analytics for data-oriented readers.

**Bookly** occupies a unique niche as a reading habit builder rather than a social cataloging tool. Its real-time session timer, gamification (diamonds, Bloo mascot, achievements), and ambient sounds target readers who want motivation and accountability. It does not compete on social features.

**Libby** is not a direct competitor in book tracking but serves the adjacent library lending market. Its ebook/audiobook reader, Kindle integration, and accessibility features are best-in-class for library patrons. It has zero tracking/cataloging ambition beyond tags and borrowing history.

**Literal** differentiates through minimalist design, human-powered recommendations (trust over algorithms), open API/extensibility, and modern social features (in-app chat, camera highlight scanning). It is the smallest platform but appeals to design-conscious readers who value community over scale.
