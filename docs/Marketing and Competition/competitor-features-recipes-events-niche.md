### 1. NYT Cooking

**Category:** Premium Recipe Platform
**Publisher:** The New York Times Company
**Downloads:** ~100K/month
**Pricing:** $5/month or $40/year (standalone, separate from NYT News)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web

#### Recipe Database and Discovery

- **Curated Recipe Library:** Over 20,000 tested recipes from NYT editors, professional chefs, and contributors. Every recipe is tested for accuracy and deliciousness before publication.
- **Editorial Curation:** Content from recognized culinary personalities including Samin Nosrat, Ina Garten, Melissa Clark, Eric Kim, and Sam Sifton.
- **Advanced Search and Filtering:** Find recipes by diet (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free), cuisine type, meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert), and cooking time.
- **Editor's Collections:** 125+ recipe collections curated by NYT editors for themes like "Fast Dinners for Hungry, Busy People" and "Recipes for a Small Thanksgiving."
- **Daily New Content:** New recipes and videos published daily, with seasonal and trending recommendations.
- **Personalized Recommendations:** The app learns your preferences based on saved recipes and suggests new ones based on what is in season near you.

#### Recipe Box and Organization

- **Digital Recipe Box:** Save and organize unlimited recipes in your personal Recipe Box, accessible across all platforms (iOS, Android, web).
- **Drag-and-Drop Folders:** Drag and drop recipes into custom folders for easy organization.
- **Cross-Platform Sync:** Recipes saved on one device are available on all devices.

#### Cooking Tools

- **Always-On Screen:** The screen stays on while viewing a recipe so it does not go dark while you are cooking.
- **Multi-Window Support (iPad):** Keep multiple windows open for simpler cooking, especially on iPad.
- **High-Resolution Media:** Watch cooking videos and view photos on a larger screen with full resolution.

#### Video Content

- **Step-by-Step Demonstrations:** Hundreds of short-form cooking videos with guides.
- **Longform Shows:** Full cooking shows like "Cooking 101" and "The Veggie."
- **Technique Videos and Guides:** Library of technique videos teaching fundamentals (knife skills, pasta-making, etc.).

#### Grocery and Meal Planning

- **Built-In Grocery List:** Choose recipes you plan to cook, then organize all ingredients into one list.
- **Instacart Integration:** Skip manual shopping and order grocery delivery directly via Instacart.
- **Weekly Meal Organization:** Plan meals for the week using saved recipes.

#### Community Features

- **Recipe Notes (Community):** Each recipe has a Notes section where fellow subscribers share favorite ingredient swaps, tips, and modifications. Moderated by NYT staff.
- **Personal Notes:** Add your own private notes to any recipe, turning the app into a personal, evolving cookbook.
- **Ratings and Reviews:** Thousands of ratings and reviews from other home cooks on each recipe.

#### Additional Features

- **Cooking Guides:** Comprehensive guides for novices and experienced home cooks covering techniques, tips, and tricks.
- **iPad Optimized:** High-resolution experience on iPad with multi-window support.

### 2. Allrecipes

**Category:** User-Generated Recipe Community
**Publisher:** Dotdash Meredith
**Active Users:** 40M+
**Pricing:** Free (ad-supported), optional ad-free subscription
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web

#### Recipe Database and Discovery

- **Massive Recipe Library:** Tens of thousands of user-submitted and tested recipes for every conceivable dish, diet, and occasion.
- **Advanced Search and Filtering:** Filter recipes by ingredients, dietary needs (gluten-free, keto, vegan), cooking time, and user ratings.
- **Dinner Spinner:** Signature discovery feature where you select a dish type, available ingredients, and cooking time to receive randomized recipe suggestions from 50,000+ recipes.
- **Ingredient-Based Search:** Find recipes based on ingredients you already have on hand.

#### Cooking Tools

- **Cook-Friendly View (Cooking Mode):** Prevents screen from dimming and presents instructions in an easy-to-follow, step-by-step format.
- **Personal Notes:** Add private notes to saved recipes for cooking adjustments, secret tricks, and personal modifications.

#### Meal Planning

- **Built-In Meal Planner:** Calendar tool that lets you schedule recipes for the week, assign recipes to specific dates and meal types.
- **Ingredient Consolidation:** Automatically consolidates ingredients from multiple scheduled recipes into a unified shopping list.

#### Grocery and Shopping

- **Integrated Shopping List:** Add recipe ingredients with a single tap.
- **Smart Aisle Sorting:** Automatically sorts items by grocery store aisle (Produce, Meat, Dairy, etc.) to make shopping faster.
- **Intelligent Unit Conversion:** Converts recipe measurements into buyable quantities (e.g., "3/4 cup diced onion" becomes "2 onions"; "1 cup frozen peas" becomes "1 (10 oz) package frozen green peas").
- **Manual Item Entry:** Add non-recipe items to the shopping list manually.
- **Check-Off Functionality:** Check off items while shopping.

#### Recipe Organization

- **Recipe Box:** Save favorite recipes and organize them into custom collections (e.g., "Quick Weeknight Dinners," "Holiday Baking").
- **Custom Collections:** Create unlimited categorized collections for personal organization.

#### Community Features

- **User Reviews and Ratings:** Star ratings and written reviews from millions of home cooks.
- **Helpful Modifications:** Reviewers share ingredient substitutions, cooking tips, and modifications that worked.
- **Success Photos:** Users upload photos of their finished dishes to show real-world results.
- **Recipe Submission:** Community members can submit their own recipes for others to try.

#### Pricing and Premium

- **Free Tier:** Full access to all recipes, shopping list, and core features with ads.
- **Ad-Free Subscription:** Premium "Cook's Notes" subscription removes advertisements for a cleaner experience.

### 3. Paprika Recipe Manager

**Category:** Privacy-First Recipe Manager
**Publisher:** Hindsight Labs LLC
**Pricing:** One-time purchase per platform (iOS: $4.99, Android: $4.99, macOS: $29.99, Windows: $29.99; frequently on sale at 50% off)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, macOS, Windows

#### Recipe Management

- **Web Recipe Import:** Built-in browser lets you browse any recipe website and save recipes with one tap. Automatically extracts title, ingredients, directions, and photos.
- **Browser Bookmarklet:** Download recipes from any browser straight into your Paprika Cloud Sync account without opening the app.
- **Manual Recipe Entry:** Create recipes from scratch with full formatting support.
- **Rich Text Formatting:** Add bold, italic, and links to recipe text.
- **Multiple Images:** Store multiple high-resolution images per recipe in your cloud account.
- **Recipe Import/Export:** Import recipes from other desktop and mobile apps.

#### Cooking Tools

- **Auto-Scaling Ingredients:** Automatically scale ingredients up or down to match your desired serving size.
- **Measurement Conversion:** Convert ingredients between metric and imperial measurements.
- **Auto-Detected Timers:** Timers are automatically detected in directions; simply tap on a time to start a countdown timer.
- **Pin Active Recipes:** Pin multiple active recipes to easily switch between them when cooking multiple dishes simultaneously.
- **Ingredient Cross-Off:** Track progress by crossing off ingredients as you use them.
- **Direction Step Highlighting:** Highlight your current direction step to keep your place.
- **Screen Stay-On:** Screen stays on while viewing a recipe, so it does not go dark while cooking.

#### Grocery Lists

- **Auto-Sorted Aisles:** Ingredients are automatically sorted into grocery store aisles and consolidated.
- **Intelligent Consolidation:** Similar ingredients from different recipes are combined (e.g., "1 egg + 2 eggs = 3 eggs").
- **Multiple Grocery Lists:** Create and manage multiple separate grocery lists.
- **Customizable Aisles:** Organize and customize grocery aisle categories.

#### Meal Planning

- **Weekly/Monthly Meal Plans:** Create a different meal plan for every week or month.
- **Reusable Menus:** Design reusable menus from your favorite meals, then apply them to different weeks.

#### Pantry Management

- **Pantry Tracker:** Monitor groceries you have on hand.
- **Expiration Date Tracking:** Set expiration dates for pantry items to reduce food waste.

#### Organization and Search

- **Categories and Subcategories:** Organize recipes into hierarchical categories and subcategories.
- **Multi-Field Search:** Search by recipe name, source, ingredient, or any other field.
- **Linked Recipes:** Link to recipes from the ingredients panel. Clicking a linked ingredient navigates to that recipe, then you can browse back.

#### Cloud and Sync

- **Paprika Cloud Sync:** Seamlessly sync recipes, grocery lists, and meal plans across all devices. Included with purchase, no extra fees.
- **Offline Access:** All data is stored locally; no internet connection required to view recipes.

#### Sharing and Export

- **Email Sharing:** Share recipes via email.
- **Print Support:** Print recipes, grocery lists, menus, and meal plans. Recipes support multiple print formats including index cards.

#### Pricing Model

- **One-Time Purchase:** Each platform version is sold separately with no subscription required.
- **Free Cloud Sync:** Cloud sync is included with purchase at no additional cost.
- **No Ads:** The app contains no advertisements.

### 4. Tasty

**Category:** Video-First Recipe Content Platform
**Publisher:** BuzzFeed
**Users:** 25M+
**Pricing:** Free (ad-supported)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android

#### Recipe Database and Discovery

- **Massive Library:** 10,000+ recipes with video guides and step-by-step instructions.
- **Multi-Criteria Search:** Search recipes by social plans, ingredients, dietary needs, difficulty, speed, cuisine, and more.
- **Dietary Filters:** Filter by vegan, gluten-free, low carb, healthy, comfort food, and other categories.
- **Ingredient Search:** Find dishes based on as few as 3 ingredients you have on hand.
- **Contextual Recommendations:** Personalized suggestions based on time of day, day of the week, major holidays, and seasonal trends.

#### Video Content

- **Step-by-Step Video Tutorials:** Short-form instructional videos demonstrating every step of recipe preparation.
- **Video Previews:** Visual demonstration of what finished dishes should look like.
- **Creator Content:** Recipes and cooking secrets from featured Tasty Creators.

#### AI Features

- **Botatouille (AI Culinary Companion):** ChatGPT-powered assistant that answers cooking questions in real-time ("What recipes can I make with ingredients in my fridge?" or "How long do you cook salmon?"). Currently iOS-only.
- **AI Meal Planning:** Botatouille can assist with planning weekly meals and discovering recipes based on available ingredients.
- **Recipe Remixes:** AI-powered feature that lets you make any recipe easier, more nutritious, or turn it into something unique. Alternative versions contributed by community and Tasty creators.

#### Cooking Tools

- **Step-by-Step Cooking Mode:** Interactive guidance through recipes with always-on screen.
- **Adjustable Serving Sizes:** Scale serving sizes with metric and US measurements displayed side by side.
- **Community Tips and Ratings:** User-contributed cooking advice and ratings on each recipe.

#### Shopping and Grocery

- **Shoppable Recipes (Walmart Integration):** Add the entire ingredient list from 4,000+ Tasty recipes directly to a Walmart Online Grocery cart with a single click.
- **Grocery Delivery/Pickup:** Seamless checkout with Walmart for pickup from 2,500+ stores or delivery from 1,100+ stores.
- **Shopping Lists:** Compile ingredient lists from selected recipes.

#### Personalization

- **Dietary Preferences:** Personalize the app to automatically hide recipes with meat or other unwanted ingredients.
- **Custom Cookbooks:** Create personal collections of saved favorite recipes organized by theme.
- **Recipe Saving:** Bookmark recipes to your profile for easy access later.

#### Community and Social

- **Tasty Community:** Platform for sharing cooking photos and creations with other users.
- **Creator Interaction:** Follow and interact with featured Tasty Creators.
- **Community Tips:** User-generated advice and cooking guidance on recipes.
- **Recipe Sharing:** Share dishes with other community members.

### 5. Partiful

**Category:** Gen-Z Event/RSVP Platform
**Publisher:** Partiful Inc.
**MAU:** 500K+ (Google Play Best App 2024)
**Pricing:** 100% Free (no premium tier)
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web (no account required for guests)

#### Event Creation

- **Instant Event Pages:** Create event pages in seconds with customizable titles, date/time, location, and descriptions.
- **Event Type Versatility:** Support for birthdays, pregames, kickbacks, dinners, date nights, game nights, watch parties, pickup games, group trips, bachelor/bachelorettes, weddings, and more.
- **Event Posters:** Choose from a poster picker that searches by event type (birthday, housewarming, picnic, happy hour) or vibe (chill, summer, outdoors), or upload your own photo/GIF.
- **Themes and Effects:** Customize event pages with background themes and animated effects using Theme and Effects sidebars.
- **Font Selection:** Choose from multiple font options for your event page.
- **Image Upload:** Upload custom images or use templates for event pages.

#### RSVP and Guest Management

- **Frictionless RSVP:** Guests receive a link, enter their phone number, and RSVP instantly without downloading an app or creating an account.
- **Guest List Visibility:** Attendees can see who else is going to the event.
- **Maximum Guest Limits:** Set a capacity cap for your event.
- **Automatic Waitlist:** Enable a waitlist when capacity is reached. If a spot opens, the next waitlisted guest is automatically added and notified via text.
- **Plus-One Permissions:** Configure whether guests can bring additional people.
- **RSVP Approval Settings:** Option for manual confirmation so hosts approve each RSVP.
- **Custom Questions:** Collect custom information from guests during RSVP (dietary preferences, etc.).
- **Boops:** A reaction system that lets guests react to RSVPs with playful acknowledgments.
- **Guest Bios:** Integration capability for guest bios.

#### Communication

- **Text Blasts:** Message all confirmed guests at once (door codes, delays, reminders, "bring something" alerts).
- **Automatic Reminders:** Automated reminder notifications sent to guests before the event.
- **Activity Log:** Comment sections and activity feeds on the event page.
- **Guest Replies:** Guests can reply to host messages.
- **Notification Controls:** Customize which notifications you receive (RSVP alerts, messages).

#### Co-Hosting

- **Co-Host Support:** Add co-hosts from your Partiful mutuals (people you have partied with before) or past/future guests. Co-hosts share event management capabilities.

#### Scheduling and Polls

- **Find a Time Polls:** Write in dates or times that work; guests vote Yes, No, or Maybe. When you pick a time, all guest responses automatically convert into RSVPs.
- **Event Duplication:** Duplicate any past event and re-invite the same guests for recurring gatherings.

#### Photos and Social

- **Shared Photo Album:** Guests can upload and view photos from the event.
- **Comments and Reactions:** Interactive commenting and reaction system on event pages.

#### Payments and Integrations

- **Payment Integration:** Connect Venmo, Cash App, or PayPal for collecting money from guests.
- **Social Media Handle Collection:** Collect Instagram and Twitter handles from guests.
- **Calendar Sync:** Automatically add Partiful events to Google, Apple, or Outlook calendars with customizable sync preferences.
- **Multiple Sharing Options:** Share events via copied link, WhatsApp, Facebook, email, or text message.
- **Canva Workflow:** Power users create invitation graphics in Canva, then upload to Partiful.

### 6. Eventbrite

**Category:** Event Creation and Ticketing Platform
**Publisher:** Eventbrite Inc.
**Revenue:** $290M+
**Pricing:** Free to publish events; ticketing fees on paid tickets (2.9% + 3.7% + $1.79/ticket); Pro plans from $15/month
**Platforms:** iOS (Organizer App), Android, Web

- **Streamlined Setup Wizard:** Create events in minutes with guided event information entry.
- **Event Types:** Support for single, recurring, and online/virtual events.
- **AI-Powered Descriptions:** AI generates event descriptions, social media copy, and email content 30% faster.
- **Custom Event Pages:** Customizable event URLs with multiple image uploads and embedded video.
- **Agendas and Speaker Lineups:** Add detailed schedules and speaker information.
- **Marketplace Listing:** Events listed on the Eventbrite discovery marketplace for organic reach.
- **Collections:** Curate groups of related events onto a single page for cross-promotion.

#### Ticketing and Registration

- **Multiple Ticket Types:** Create tiers such as early bird, general admission, VIP, and group tickets with different pricing.
- **Early Bird Tickets:** Encourage early registration with special time-limited pricing.
- **Group Tickets:** Offer bulk purchase discounts for group registration.
- **Reserved Seating:** Full seating chart maker where attendees select specific seats at purchase time.
- **Timed Entry:** Set up time slots, scan tickets at the door, and control crowd flow.
- **Custom Tickets:** Create branded printable and PDF tickets.
- **Embedded Checkout:** Allow ticket purchases directly on your own website.
- **Promo Codes:** Create percentage or fixed-amount discount codes for special access.
- **Hidden/Password-Protected Tickets:** Tickets accessible only via password for exclusive access.
- **Scheduled Ticket Sales:** Set tickets to go on sale at specific future times.
- **Purchase Quantity Limits:** Set maximum and minimum purchase quantities per transaction.
- **Waitlist:** Enable waitlists for sold-out events with automatic notification when spots open.
- **Customizable Registration Forms:** Collect specific attendee information during registration.
- **Custom Order Confirmation Emails:** Personalized purchase confirmation messages.

#### On-Site Event Management

- **Eventbrite Organizer App:** Mobile app for day-of event management.
- **QR Code Scanning:** Quick check-in by scanning attendee QR codes.
- **Manual Attendee Lookup:** Search by name or email for manual check-in.
- **Real-Time Attendance Tracking:** Live dashboard showing check-in progress.
- **At-the-Door Ticket Sales:** Sell tickets at the event with credit card processing.
- **Offline Functionality:** Check-in works offline with data syncing when reconnected.
- **Tap to Pay:** Contactless payment processing at the door.

#### Marketing and Promotion

- **Eventbrite Ads:** Premium placements with 14x more impressions.
- **Email Marketing Tools:** Send emails with no contact limits (daily limits by tier: 250 free, up to 10,000 on Pro).
- **Smart Audience Targeting:** Use exclusive Eventbrite attendee data for targeted campaigns.
- **Facebook Ads Integration:** Smart budget reallocation to engaged audiences, direct ticket purchasing from Facebook.
- **Instagram Ads:** Launch ads to amplify reach.
- **TikTok Integration:** Feature events on TikTok user feeds with integrated ticketing.
- **LinkedIn Integration:** Share events directly on LinkedIn.
- **Automated Notifications:** Automatic notifications sent to your followers when you publish new events.

#### Analytics and Reporting

- **Attendee Summary Reports:** Complete registration lists with contact information.
- **Sales Reports:** Revenue breakdown by ticket type, channel, and time period.
- **Traffic and Conversion Analysis:** Identify which marketing efforts drive the most ticket sales.
- **Tracking Pixels:** Meta and Google pixel integration for ad performance measurement.
- **Tracking Links:** Measure individual link success for different marketing channels.
- **Real-Time Dashboards:** Live sales and check-in monitoring dashboards.
- **CSV Export:** Export all reports to CSV for external analysis.
- **Post-Event Follow-Up Tools:** Communication tools for attendee engagement after events.

#### Payments

- **Secure Payment Processing:** Credit and debit card processing.
- **Instant Payouts:** Withdraw funds from ticket sales within minutes (vs. standard 3-day payout).
- **Flexible Fee Handling:** Organizers can absorb ticketing fees or pass them to attendees.

#### Virtual Events and Integrations

- **Zoom Integration:** Automatic sync of event details for virtual events.
- **Facebook Live Integration:** Stream virtual events on Facebook.
- **YouTube Integration:** Host virtual events via YouTube.
- **API Access:** Full API for custom integrations.
- **Third-Party CRM Compatibility:** Connect to external CRM and email marketing systems.

#### Additional Features

- **Merchandise and Add-Ons:** Sell t-shirts, VIP experiences, and other merchandise alongside tickets.
- **Sponsorship and Exhibitor Management:** Tools for managing event sponsors and exhibitors.
- **Surveys and Polls:** Create pre- and post-event surveys and interactive polls.

#### Pricing Tiers

- **Pro 2K:** $15/month -- 2,000 emails/day, 24/7 chat support, all marketing tools.

### 8. Star Walk 2

**Category:** AR Stargazing and Astronomy
**Publisher:** Vito Technology Inc.
**Downloads:** 40M+
**Pricing:** Star Walk 2 Plus (Free, ad-supported) / Star Walk 2 Pro ($2.99, ad-free) / In-app purchases $0.99 each or $5.99 all-inclusive
**Platforms:** iOS, Android

#### AR and Sky Viewing

- **Interactive Sky Map:** Real-time positions of stars, planets, and celestial objects displayed on an interactive map that moves as you move your device.
- **Augmented Reality Mode:** Overlay the sky map onto your device's camera view. Point your phone at the sky and see labels for every visible celestial object in real time.
- **Free Roam Camera Mode:** Explore the sky freely by dragging and swiping.
- **Scroll/Manual Navigation:** Navigate the sky map manually without relying on device orientation.

#### Celestial Database

- **Stars:** Database of over 200,000 stars with detailed information.
- **Planets:** All 8 planets of the solar system with 3D models.
- **Asteroids and Comets:** 27+ asteroids and comets tracked with orbital paths.
- **Satellites (Premium):** 10,000+ satellites including ISS and Starlink constellation tracking with real-time pass predictions.
- **Deep-Sky Objects (Premium):** Star clusters, nebulae, and galaxies with 3D visualizations.
- **Small Solar System Bodies (Premium):** Comets, dwarf planets, asteroids, and meteor shower tracking.
- **Spacecraft:** Track the Hubble Space Telescope and other spacecraft.

#### Identification and Information

- **Real-Time Identification:** Identify stars, constellations, planets, satellites, asteroids, comets, spacecraft, nebulae, and the ISS by pointing your device at the sky.
- **Detailed Object Information:** Tap any celestial object for detailed scientific data including mass, brightness, distance from Earth, and more.
- **Rise and Set Times:** Calculations for the Sun, Moon, and planets showing when they rise and set at your location.

#### Time Travel and Planning

- **Time Machine:** Select any past or future date and time. Watch the night sky map animate in fast motion as you scrub through time.
- **Astronomy Calendar:** Upcoming astronomical events including meteor showers, eclipses, planetary conjunctions, and other celestial phenomena.
- **Planet Walk:** Discover which planets are visible from your location tonight and find the best time to witness alignments.
- **Sky Live / Visible Tonight:** Features that help find the optimal moments to observe specific planets and constellations.

#### Educational Content

- **3D Constellation Models (Premium):** Beautifully rendered, rotatable 3D models of constellations with mythological stories and cultural context.
- **Interactive 3D Planet Models (Premium):** Detailed 3D models of planets and spacecraft you can rotate and explore.
- **Educational Cartoons (Premium):** Space education content designed for younger users and beginners.
- **Astronomy Quizzes:** Test your astronomy knowledge with interactive quizzes.
- **Infographics:** Elegant visuals that help grasp complex celestial events.
- **News Feed:** Latest news in astronomy, space exploration, and stargazing trends.

#### User Experience

- **Night Mode:** Red-tinted interface that preserves dark-adapted night vision during stargazing sessions.
- **Offline Support:** The app works without an internet connection, perfect for remote stargazing locations.
- **No Account Required:** Start using immediately without creating an account.

#### Pricing Model

- **Star Walk 2 Plus (Free):** Full app with ads. All core features included. Premium content available via in-app purchases or subscription.
- **Star Walk 2 Pro ($2.99):** One-time purchase, completely ad-free. Same features as Plus minus ads. Premium content available via separate in-app purchases.
- **Premium Add-On Packs ($0.99 each):** Deep-Sky Objects, Small Solar System Bodies, Satellite Database, Interactive 3D Models, Educational Cartoons, Ad Removal (Plus only).
- **All-Inclusive Bundle ($5.99):** One-time purchase unlocking all premium content packs plus ad removal.
- **Subscription (Plus only):** Optional auto-renewable subscription for all premium content plus ad removal. Available in monthly, annual, and one-time permanent purchase options.
- **Important:** Purchases made in the Pro version do not transfer to the Plus version, and vice versa. They are completely separate apps.
